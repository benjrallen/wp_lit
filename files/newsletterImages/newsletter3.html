Welcome to the July Lit Newsletter!

Welcome to another installment of the Lit Newsletter! As always, another exciting month here at Lit. Our team has been hard at work, with our engineers improving our prototype, our designers working on the interior of the C-1 (which we’ll show you soon!), and our marketing team exhibiting at events. We’ve also been making updates and improvements to our site – and we’re now in Wikipedia!

The most recent of these events was EV Live, an electric vehicle festival attached to the Portland, Oregon, leg of the TTXGP. We presented our C-1 model to the crowd of EV enthusiasts to an overwhelmingly positive response!

We have a new t-shirt design! As you can see on the right, these new shirts visually track the convergent evolution of two-wheeled and four-wheeled vehicles, resulting in the C-1. The shirt is available on our site, and comes in black, dark gray, and off-white heather gray, S-XL.

Our recent media coverage includes CNET, British tech blog PocketLint, German magazine Spiegel, several German and Indian blogs, and our local San Francisco ABC affiliate, KGO 7, with the television debuts of Kevin Lomeli, our controls engineer, and Ryan James, our Chief Marketing Officer!
